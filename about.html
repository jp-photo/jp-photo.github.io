Jacob Purdy is a multi-talented artist located in upstate New York. His work primarily consists of fashion photography and portraiture. Purdy’s other work includes paintings, drawings and other traditional mediums. The nature of Purdy’s photography is closely related to the world of fashion, taking inspiration from contemporary fashion movements and designers. Purdy’s portraiture seeks to demonstrate the individualized and unique identity of people through the clothes they wear and the emotions they display.

Purdy’s conceptual pieces seek to describe a highly personal vision that draws heavy inspiration from childhood experiences. This conceptual work is self-referential and reflects personal behavioural processes. Through this work, Purdy hopes to elevate his own intrapersonal relationship and achieve deeper understanding of himself.
